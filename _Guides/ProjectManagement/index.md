# Project Management

So you've finally created your first Data Foundry project, and now you dont know what to do or how to invite your group members to Data Foundry. Well fear not because you've found the right place! This section of the How-To guide is all about how you can organize and manage your projects on Data Foundry. Ranging from the basics like adding group members to your project to more advanced setups where you want to automatically invite a wide range of participants without having to set these up manually. These guides will show you how you can supercharge your Data Foundry projects and even will show you how to publish these to share knowledge and inspire others.
