# Survey Dataset
The Survey Dataset is almost the same as the Form Dataset, but where the Form Dataset is used for anonymous data collection, the survey dataset also logs the participant ID. This means that all of their responses will be associated with their participant ID. This means that you can generate personalised links for each of your participants. Something that the form dataset does not have.

For more information on how to use the survey dataset, check out the form dataset guide](/Guides/Datasets/Form/FormDataset.md).
