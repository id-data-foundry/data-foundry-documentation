# Welcome to Data Foundry

Data Foundry is an open-source data collection and prototyping platform made for design education and research. Data Foundry (DF) is an easy, safe and flexible environment to work with all things data for design prototyping and studies. Data Foundry provides you and your students the tools to collect, manage and share data. There are also various tools to make prototyping, data visualisation, website hosting and collaboration on data design easier. Whether you are building a data logger or creating your own chatbot, you have found the right platform. If Data Foundry does not offer all the tools you need we made sure to make it easy to connect to other platforms, while keeping you data stored in a safe and structured way.

## Getting Started

Over time, Data Foundry has grown into a large and capable platform, here are a few pointers to get started. Feel free to follow the links below or make use of the side navigation to jump ahead.

### Students and Researchers

Let's get you started on Data Foundry. The best way to get a hands-on experience, is to go through the [tutorials]({% link _Tutorials/index.md %}). There we give you a basic idea how to use the platform and get started with your projects.

If you want to dive deeper, follow the links at the end of each tutorial or check the [how-to guides]({% link _Guides/index.md %}) to look into how Data Foundry works. Also, familiarize yourself with the support team for your local Data Foundry instance.


### Teachers

Data Foundry is great for design education, especially when you would like to dive into data design and you don't want to develop a whole stack yourself. At the same time, Data Foundry champions local data, open-source and transparency in data design. Convinced? We have a pointers for how to integrate Data Foundry into your education or bring Data Foundry to your institution. Feel free to [contact us](mailto:info@data-foundry.net). We are happy to help you adopt Data Foundry into your education and to explore new ways to boost data design education.

### Administrators and Developers

There are useful pointers for [behind-the-scenes operations]({% link _Setup/Administrators/index.md %}) of Data Foundry such as how to install and start an instance, how to secure it and make backups, and how to support users in getting the most out of the platform. We are also looking for developers to contribute to the platform in terns of new features or improvements. You can find more information on [GitHub](https://github.com/data-foundry-id) and our [contributor guide](https://github.com/data-foundry-id/CONTRIBUTORS.md).


## Documentation overview

This documentation consists of 4 major parts;
* **Tutorials** introduce you to Data Foundry workflows. The workflows are great for getting started and learning about the main concepts of Data Foundry on the side. Here you can create your first project and datasets, host a small website or design a chatbot. We have click-through tours as well; it won't get easier than this.
* **Learning materials** explain the inner workings of Data Foundry, how projects, datasets, and resources connect. The information here is great for going deeper after the first few steps with our tutorials.
* **How-to-Guides** explain how to achieve specific goals like using Data foundry with Python, Processing or working with the built-in tools or scripts. Here you will also find guides for specific technologies or use-cases. It's worth a [browse]({% link _Guides/Clients/index.md %}).
* **Reference materials** provide lots of detail on working with Data Foundry, for instance, how to use the different APIs, how to use scripting to access platform features and even extend Data Foundry. This is advanced material but actually not hard to understanding.

We follow the Diataxis framework which divide the information in a task-specific way.
