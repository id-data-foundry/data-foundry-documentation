# Narrative Surveys

Narrative surveys in Data Foundry are a powerful tool for research, allowing you to move beyond simple question-and-answer formats. By combining the structure of a survey with the engagement of interactive storytelling, you can explore how participants make choices, understand perspectives, and navigate complex scenarios. Unlike standard forms, these surveys allow the participant to be an active agent in a story, providing a richer context for their responses.

## Interactive Stories

At their core, Narrative Surveys are interactive stories. Instead of a linear list of questions, you create branching paths where a reader actively navigates through the content. This is built upon the popular [Twine](https://twinetools.org/) format (specifically Twee), which is widely used for interactive fiction. This format allows you to create complex, non-linear structures where a participant's choices determine the flow of the narrative.

Creating a survey is straightforward and integrated directly into your workflow. Within your project, navigate to the **Narrative Surveys** tab (accessible alongside your **Timeline** and **Sources**). Here, you can create a new survey and use the built-in editor to write your passages and define the links between them. Whether you are building a simple scenario or a complex, multi-ending story, the tool provides a flexible environment to bring your research design to life.

Once your authoring is complete, the platform makes it easy to distribute your work. After testing the survey yourself to ensure the flow is correct, you can generate links to send to your participants. They can then complete the survey on their own time, with the system handling the session management. This ease of distribution makes it suitable for both remote studies and controlled lab environments.

## Data Collection

One of the distinct advantages of using Narrative Surveys in Data Foundry is the depth of data collection. Every interaction—every link clicked, every path chosen, and the time spent on each passage—is automatically recorded in a dedicated dataset. This allows you to capture not just the final outcome, but the journey the participant took to get there. This "process data" is often just as valuable as the answers themselves.

In addition to recording navigation choices, Narrative Surveys support text entry fields. This allows you to ask participants for their thoughts, reasoning, or intentions at specific points in the story. Because this is integrated into the Data Foundry ecosystem, all of this data is stored securely and structured within your project's dataset.

This granular tracking is essential for studies focusing on decision-making processes or perspective-taking. Unlike external tools where you might only get a summary csv, here the data is linked to your project's participant structure. This means you can easily correlate narrative choices with other data points in your study, such as device data or other survey responses.

## Media Integration

To make your narratives more immersive, Data Foundry allows you to embed media directly into your stories. You are not limited to text; you can include images to illustrate scenarios, provide visual cues, or simply enhance the aesthetic of the survey. A picture often conveys context much faster than a paragraph of text, which is crucial for keeping participants engaged.

On the Narrative Surveys management page, you will find an **Images and other files** section specifically for this purpose. Here, you can upload your assets (supporting formats like PNG, JPG, and GIF). Once uploaded, the system generates a secure "Embed link" for each file. You can simply copy this code snippet and paste it into your narrative passage where you want the image to appear.

Please be mindful of what you upload. As noted in the upload interface, you should avoid uploading files that contain private or sensitive data (such as personal information, medical data, or financial details) unless you have the explicit right and security clearance to do so. The system is designed to serve these assets securely to your participants, but data minimization is always a best practice in research.

## Analysis

Collecting data is only the first step; making sense of it is where the real insight happens. Data Foundry provides built-in tools to help you analyze the data generated by your Narrative Surveys without needing to immediately export it to a third-party tool.

From the survey list in your project view, you can access a dedicated **Data Analysis** dashboard for each survey. This view helps you visualize how participants moved through your story, highlighting common paths, drop-off points, and the distribution of choices. This immediate feedback loop is invaluable for piloting your study and iterating on your narrative design before launching it to a wider audience.

If you need to perform more complex statistical analysis or combine this data with other sources, you are not locked in. As with all data in the platform, you can export the raw dataset. This gives you the flexibility to use your preferred statistical software (like R, Python, or SPSS) while benefiting from the structured data collection that Data Foundry provides.
