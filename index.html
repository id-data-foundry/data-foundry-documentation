What is Data Foundry?

Data Foundry is an open-source data collection and prototyping platform made for design education and research. Data Foundry (DF) is an easy, safe, and flexible environment to work with all things data for design prototyping and studies. Data Foundry provides you and your students the tools to collect, manage, and share data. There are also various tools to make prototyping, data visualization, website hosting, and collaboration on data design easier. Whether you are building a data logger or creating your own chatbot, you have found the right platform. If Data Foundry does not offer all the tools you need for your use case, we made sure to make it easy to connect to other platforms while keeping your data stored in a safe and structured way. Data Foundry has been battle-tested for 7 years in our own design education and research, and is now freely accessible to you too!

Data Collection

Collect data from prototypes, wireframes, or the people you design for and store it in a structured format that is easy to manage and analyze. Everything is on-premise and in compliance with GDPR. We provide various data collection tools and also offer the resources to build your own!

Data Prototyping

Use the Data Foundry API to design and prototype connected applications that can access machine-learning models and speech APIs. This makes it easier to get your data from A to B. We support a wide range of programming languages and even have several libraries for embedded devices!

Joudy Bourghli & The Bigger Picture / The Omnipresent Tapestry / Licensed by CC-BY 4.0

Design Research

Data Foundry contains the tools necessary for data-related methods such as narrative surveys and object ethnography. It offers the means to export data in numerous formats and collect rich quantitative and qualitative data. From surveys to diary studies and data annotations, we have the tools to structure your research in a FAIR way!
